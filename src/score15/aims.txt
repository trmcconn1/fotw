              Syracuse University Noon Time Running Competition

                                    Aims

1. To provide interdepartmental running competition, both individual and
team, among Syracuse University faculty, staff and graduate students.

2. To offer participants an opportunity to achieve set running standards.

3. To develop a spirit of comaraderie and friendly competition among
participating runners.

4. To promote running along with all the personal, social and health
benefits that can be attained through this form of exercise.
